FAQS

Is FLBQR registered ? +

Yes, FLBQR was registered in 2019.

Does FLBQR cater for all asylum seekers / refugees ? +

Answer: No, FLBQR is focused on registered LBQGNC refugees and works closely with other LGBTIQ+ organizations.

Does FLBQR has a permanent safe house ? +

No FLBQR has a transitional home for people who are referred and there documents have been verified the longest you can stay in the transitional safe house is two weeks as you figure something out.

Does FLBQR cater for mental health? +

Yes, we for mental health through referrals of partnered organization that we have.

Does FLBQR cater for LBQ Women refugees needs like rent and food? +

No. FLBQR is not mandated to pay rent for refugees/ asylum seekers or buy them food.

Does FLBQR help out in Emergency situations for example taking sick people to hospitals and paying their hospital bills? +

No. FLBQR does refer patients to NCCK HUDUMA hospitals for further assessment and referrals.
